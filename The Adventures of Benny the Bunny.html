Once upon a time, in a sunny meadow, there lived a little bunny named Benny. Benny loved to hop around and explore. One day, he found a beautiful butterfly named Bella. She had wings of sparkly colors and loved to fly.

Benny asked Bella, "Can you teach me how to fly?" Bella laughed and said, "Bunnies can't fly, but I can take you on a magical adventure!"

Together, they hopped and flew over flowers, crossed a sparkling stream, and met a friendly squirrel named Squeaky. Squeaky shared some yummy acorns, and they all had a picnic.

As the sun began to set, Benny said, "This was the best day ever! Thank you, Bella!" Bella smiled and said, "You're welcome, little friend. Let's do it again soon!"

The end.
