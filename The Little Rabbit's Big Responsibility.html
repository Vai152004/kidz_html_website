Once upon a time, in a forest, a little rabbit named Rosie was given a big responsibility. She was tasked with watering her family's garden every day. Rosie was excited but also worried. "What if I forget?" she thought.

Every day, Rosie diligently watered the plants. She watched them grow and flourish. But one day, she got distracted playing with her friends and forgot to water the garden.

The next day, the plants began to wilt. Rosie's parents were disappointed. Rosie felt terrible and promised to never forget again. She worked extra hard to revive the plants.

Rosie learned that taking responsibility is important and that mistakes can be fixed with effort and determination. From then on, she remembered to water the garden every day, and it thrived.

अगले दिन, पौधे मुरझाने लगे। रोजी के माता-पिता निराश हुए। रोजी को बहुत बुरा लगा और उसने वादा किया कि वह फिर कभी नहीं भूलेगी। उसने पौधों को फिर से स्वस्थ करने के लिए कड़ी मेहनत की।
