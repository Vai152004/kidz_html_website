In a virtual world, two friends, Ava and Max, created their avatars to play games together. Ava's avatar was kind and helpful, while Max's avatar was mean and cheated.

Other players started to avoid Max's avatar, but Ava's avatar was loved by all. Max realized his avatar's behavior was hurting others and changed it to be kind and fair.

The virtual world became a happier place, and Max learned that kindness and fairness are essential, even in the digital world.
